# Community
Hunter College Computer Science Professor Reviews

# Sources
- Rate My Professors
- Reddit (r/HunterCollege)

# Labels

## helpful_review
Reviews that describe a professor's teaching style, assignments, grading, workload, or course structure.

## course_advice
Reviews that provide recommendations or warnings to future students.

## emotional_reaction
Reviews that primarily express positive or negative feelings about a professor or course.

# Goal
Train a classifier that predicts whether a review is a helpful review, course advice, or an emotional reaction.
